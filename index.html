    Grammatical Categories: A Comparative Overview with Examples

    Noun (Съществително име, Substantiv, Nom, Noun)

    • Bulgarian: съществително име
      • Proper noun (именително съществително име): Иван (Ivan), София (Sofia)
      • Common noun (нарицателно съществително име): книга (book), стол (chair)
      • Concrete noun (определително съществително име): ябълка (apple), къща (house)
      • Abstract noun (неопределително съществително име): любов (love), щастие (happiness)
      • Collective noun (събирателно съществително име): стадо (herd), гора (forest)
    • German: Substantiv
      • Eigenname: Peter, Berlin
      • Gattungsname: Buch, Stuhl
      • Konkretes Substantiv: Apfel, Haus
      • Abstraktes Substantiv: Liebe, Glück
      • Kollektivum: Herde, Wald
    • French: nom
      • nom propre: Pierre, Paris
      • nom commun: livre, chaise
      • nom concret: pomme, maison
      • nom abstrait: amour, bonheur
      • nom collectif: troupeau, forêt
    • English: noun
      • proper noun: John, London
      • common noun: book, chair
      • concrete noun: apple, house
      • abstract noun: love, happiness
      • collective noun: herd, forest

    Verb (Глагол, Verb, Verbe, Verb)

    • Bulgarian: глагол
      • Main verb (глаголно сказуемо): играя (I play), чета (I read)
      • Auxiliary verb (спомагателен глагол): съм (to be), имам (to have)
      • Modal verb (модален глагол): мога (can), трябва (must)
      • Infinitive (безкраен глагол): играти (to play), чете (to read)
      • Participle (причастие): играл (played), четен (read)
      • Gerund (действително причастие): играейки (playing), четейки (reading)
    • German: Verb
      • Vollverb: spielen, lesen
      • Hilfsverb: sein, haben
      • Modalverb: können, müssen
      • Infinitiv: spielen, lesen
      • Partizip: gespielt, gelesen
      • Gerundium: Spielend, lesend
    • French: verbe
      • verbe principal: jouer, lire
      • verbe auxiliaire: être, avoir
      • verbe modal: pouvoir, devoir
      • verbe à l'infinitif: jouer, lire
      • participe: joué, lu
      • gérondif: jouant, lisant
    • English: verb
      • main verb: play, read
      • auxiliary verb: be, have
      • modal verb: can, must
      • infinitive: to play, to read
      • participle: played, read
      • gerund: playing, reading

    Adjective (Прилагателно име, Adjektiv, Adjectif, Adjective)

    • Bulgarian: прилагателно име
      • Attributive adjective (прилагателно име в съгласуване): червена ябълка (red apple), голяма къща (big house)
      • Predicative adjective (прилагателно име в несъгласуване): ябълката е червена (the apple is red), къщата е голяма (the house is big)
      • Descriptive adjective (описателно прилагателно име): красива (beautiful), интересна (interesting)
      • Qualitative adjective (качествено прилагателно име): топъл (warm), студен (cold)
      • Quantitative adjective (количествено прилагателно име): много (many), два (two)
    • German: Adjektiv
      • Attributives Adjektiv: roter Apfel, großes Haus
      • Prädikatives Adjektiv: Der Apfel ist rot, Das Haus ist groß.
      • Deskriptives Adjektiv: schön, interessant
      • Qualitatives Adjektiv: warm, kalt
      • Quantitatives Adjektiv: viel, zwei
    • French: adjectif
      • adjectif qualificatif: pomme rouge, grande maison
      • adjectif possessif: ma pomme (my apple), ton livre (your book)
      • adjectif démonstratif: cette maison (this house), ces livres (these books)
      • adjectif interrogatif: quel livre (which book)?
      • adjectif indéfini: quelques pommes (some apples), beaucoup de livres (many books)
    • English: adjective
      • attributive adjective: red apple, big house
      • predicative adjective: The apple is red, The house is big.
      • descriptive adjective: beautiful, interesting
      • qualitative adjective: warm, cold
      • quantitative adjective: many, two

    Adverb (Наречие, Adverb, Adverbe, Adverb)

    • Bulgarian: наречие
      • Manner adverb (начинно наречие): бързо (quickly), добре (well)
      • Place adverb (место наречие): тук (here), там (there)
      • Time adverb (временно наречие): сега (now), вчера (yesterday)
      • Degree adverb (степенно наречие): много (very), малко (little)
      • Frequency adverb (честотно наречие): често (often), рядко (rarely)
    • German: Adverb
      • Adverb der Art und Weise: schnell, gut
      • Adverb des Ortes: hier, dort
      • Adverb der Zeit: jetzt, gestern
      • Adverb des Grades: sehr, wenig
      • Adverb der Häufigkeit: oft, selten
    • French: adverbe
      • adverbe de manière: rapidement, bien
      • adverbe de lieu: ici, là
      • adverbe de temps: maintenant, hier
      • adverbe de degré: très, peu
      • adverbe de fréquence: souvent, rarement
    • English: adverb
      • adverb of manner: quickly, well
      • adverb of place: here, there
      • adverb of time: now, yesterday
      • adverb of degree: very, little
      • adverb of frequency: often, rarely

    Pronoun (Местоимение, Pronomen, Pronom, Pronoun)

    • Bulgarian: местоимение
      • Personal pronoun (лично местоимение): аз (I), ти (you), той (he), тя (she), ние (we), вие (you), те (they)
      • Demonstrative pronoun (указателно местоимение): този (this), този (that), тези (these), онези (those)
      • Possessive pronoun (притежателно местоимение): мой (my), твой (your), негов (his), неин (her), наш (our), ваш (your), техен (their)
      • Reflexive pronoun (възвратно местоимение): се (self)
      • Intensive pronoun (усилвателно местоимение): самият (himself, herself, itself)
      • Relative pronoun (относително местоимение): който (who, which), когото (whom), който (whose)
      • Interrogative pronoun (въпросително местоимение): кой (who), какво (what), къде (where)
      • Indefinite pronoun (неопределително местоимение): някой (someone), нещо (something), няколко (several)
    • German: Pronomen
      • Personalpronomen: ich, du, er, sie, wir, ihr, sie
      • Demonstrativpronomen: dieser, jener, diese, jene
      • Possessivpronomen: mein, dein, sein, ihr, unser, euer, ihr
      • Reflexivpronomen: sich
      • Intensivpronomen: selbst
      • Relativpronomen: der, die, das, welcher, welche, welches
      • Interrogativpronomen: wer, was, wo
      • Indefinitpronomen: jemand, etwas, einige
    • French: pronom
      • pronom personnel: je, tu, il, elle, nous, vous, ils, elles
      • pronom démonstratif: ce, cet, cette, ces
      • pronom possessif: mon, ton, son, notre, votre, leur
      • pronom réfléchi: se
      • pronom intensif: même
      • pronom relatif: qui, que, dont
      • pronom interrogatif: qui, que, où
      • pronom indéfini: quelqu'un, quelque chose, quelques-uns
    • English: pronoun
      • personal pronoun: I, you, he, she, we, you, they
      • demonstrative pronoun: this, that, these, those
      • possessive pronoun: my, your, his, her, our, your, their reflexive pronoun: self intensive pronoun: self relative pronoun: who, which, whose interrogative pronoun: who, what, where indefinite pronoun: someone, something, several

    Preposition (Предлог, Präposition, Préposition, Preposition)

    • Bulgarian: предлог
      • Simple preposition (прост предлог): в (in), на (on), за (for), с (with)
      • Compound preposition (сложен предлог): благодарение на (thanks to), поради (due to)
    • German: Präposition
      • Einfache Präposition: in, auf, für, mit
      • Zusammengesetzte Präposition: dank, wegen
    • French: préposition
      • préposition simple: dans, sur, pour, avec
      • préposition composée: grâce à, en raison de
    • English: preposition
      • simple preposition: in, on, for, with
      • compound preposition: thanks to, due to

    Conjunction (Съюз, Konjunktion, Conjonction, Conjunction)

    • Bulgarian: съюз
      • Coordinating conjunction (съчинителен съюз): и (and), или (or), но (but), а (and)
      • Subordinating conjunction (подчинителен съюз): че (that), защото (because), когато (when)
    • German: Konjunktion
      • Koordinierende Konjunktion: und, oder, aber, sondern
      • Subordinierende Konjunktion: dass, weil, wenn
    • French: conjonction
      • conjonction de coordination: et, ou, mais, et
      • conjonction de subordination: que, parce que, quand
    • English: conjunction
      • coordinating conjunction: and, or, but, and
      • subordinating conjunction: that, because, when

    Interjection (Междуметие, Interjektion, Interjection, Interjection)

    • Bulgarian: междуметие
      • Emotional interjection (емоционално междуметие): ох (ouch), ура (hurray)
      • Sound-imitative interjection (звукоподражателно междуметие): мяу (meow), кряк (quack)
    • German: Interjektion
      • Gefühlsausdruck: au, hurra
      • Lautmalerei: miau, quak
    • French: interjection
      • interjection émotionnelle: oups, hourra
      • interjection onomatopéique: miaou, coin-coin
    • English: interjection
      • emotional interjection: ouch, hooray
      • sound-imitative interjection: meow, quack
